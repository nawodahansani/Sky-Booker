Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1979, SriLankan Airlines has grown to become a respected name in global aviation. As the flag carrier of Sri Lanka, we take pride in connecting the beautiful island nation to the world, offering an authentic taste of Sri Lankan hospitality from the moment you step on board.

Our commitment to excellence, safety, and passenger comfort has earned us numerous accolades throughout our journey. Today, we operate a modern fleet of aircraft to over 60 destinations across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and beyond.

What sets us apart is our dedication to authentic Sri Lankan hospitality – warm, personal, and attentive service that reflects the very best of our island heritage.
